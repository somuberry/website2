A few short films, live action and animation, that I've created over the course of the last few years.

206 Bharati Kunj (Alt: My Animals and Other Families)

A short documentary film that watches my family go about their lives during the 2020 lockdown. Shot entirely using a 50mm lens on a Canon 70D body, with an underlying focus on the theme of 'water'.

Trapped

An animated short film exploring the struggles of farmers in drought-stricken Eastern Maharashtra, created in conversation with my uncle, a farmer in the Solapur district.

Eyes Wide Open

A goofy film made with friends about a 20 year old 'child' who reacts to a mysterious object by, against all conventional wisdom, putting it in his mouth.

The Uncertainty Principle

A collection of moving paintings, following the early-morning thoughts of a protaginst as he ponders Heisenberg's Uncertainty Principle. First conceptualized during high school, when I was attempting to comprehend this phenomenon.
